# Google Business Profile — content brief

This is the **single biggest trust signal on a cold call.** When the prospect Googles your business name (and they will, mid-call), they see:
- A blue verified panel on the right side of search
- A star rating, reviews, hours, phone
- A "Visit website" button to your site

Without this, you look like a random LLC. With it, you look like a real local business.

Time: ~30 min to submit. Verification: 1–2 weeks (Google mails a postcard with a PIN).

---

## You'll need

- A real US address. **Yes, even for an online-only business.** Use:
  - Your home address (Google lets you hide it from public listing — set "service area" instead of "address shown")
  - A UPS Store mailbox ($15/mo) — they accept Google verification postcards
  - A coworking-space address if you have one
- A real phone number (the one from step 3 of the launch runbook).
- A real business email (`hello@northbeamweb.com`).

---

## Step 1 — Create the profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) → sign in with your Google Workspace account (`hello@northbeamweb.com`).
2. Click **Add your business to Google**.

---

## Step 2 — Fill in these exact values

**Business name:** `Northbeam Web`
> Do not append "Web Design Pittsburgh" or anything else. Google bans keyword-stuffed names and will suspend the listing.

**Business category (primary):** `Website designer`

**Additional categories** (add all of these):
- `Web hosting company`
- `Internet marketing service`
- `Marketing consultant`

**Service area:** check "I deliver goods and services to my customers"
> Hide your address from the public listing. Add the cities or zip codes you'll target.
> Example: Pittsburgh, PA; Cleveland, OH; Columbus, OH — or whatever your cold-call radius is.

**Address (private):** your home / UPS box. Hidden from the listing, only used by Google for verification.

**Phone:** your business number.

**Website:** `https://northbeamweb.com`

**Hours:**
- Mon–Fri: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Sat: Closed
- Sun: Closed
- (Optional: Special hours for major holidays)

**Appointment URL:** your Cal.com booking link, e.g., `https://cal.com/dan-northbeam/15min`

## Step 5 — Photos (upload at minimum)

Google ranks profiles with photos significantly higher than those without.

- **Logo** (square, min 250×250). Use the Northbeam "N" mark or your wordmark. Generate one at [looka.com](https://looka.com) ($20) or commission one on Fiverr ($30) — don't skip this.
- **Cover photo** (landscape, min 1024×575). A clean shot of you at a laptop, or an abstract textured image.
- **At least 3 "team" photos** — even just one photo of you, taken 3 times in different settings/outfits, works.
- **At least 3 work samples** — screenshots of your concept rebuilds (the BeforeAfter cards on /work).

---

## Step 6 — Verification

Google mails a postcard to your address with a 5-digit PIN. Takes 5–14 business days. Once you receive it:

1. Go back to business.google.com.
2. Enter the PIN.
3. Listing goes live within minutes.

**Until you're verified, the listing doesn't appear in Google search.** Plan around this — don't start cold-calling the week you submit; start the week after the postcard arrives.

---

## Step 7 — Get your first 5 reviews fast

You cannot fake these. But:

1. After your first paying client launches, **ask them directly:** "Would you mind leaving a quick Google review? Takes 30 seconds, it makes a huge difference for me." Most will say yes.
2. Send them the direct review link from your GBP dashboard (Home → Get more reviews → Share review form).
3. Goal: 5 reviews in the first 60 days. That's the threshold where you stop looking new.

Pro tip: every email signature for the first year should include `Like working with us? Leave a review: [link]`.

---

## Maintenance

- **Reply to every review within 24 hours**, positive or negative. Google ranks responsive businesses higher.
- **Post weekly** in the "Updates" section (a new project shipped, a tip about website speed, etc.). 80% of small businesses never post — doing this puts you ahead.
- **Update hours for holidays** so you don't get a "closed" badge on your profile mid-day.
